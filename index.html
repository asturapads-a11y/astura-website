About Astura

An East African company, building what the region needs.

Astura is an early-stage East African manufacturing company focused on making essential women's health products available, affordable, and made in the region itself.

We launch with sanitary pads produced in Dire Dawa, Ethiopia. They're designed for comfort, priced for access, and packaged in Amharic and Somali for the women who actually use them. Local manufacturing means lower prices, more jobs, and a supply chain that doesn't break when borders close.

Over time, Astura will expand into adjacent products women and families need every day: diapers, feminine care, and broader hygiene goods. All locally made, locally distributed, and locally owned.

Our Approach

Four pillars that guide every decision.

Producing pads in Dire Dawa to reduce costs, shorten supply chains, and keep value inside the communities we serve.

Ask Astura

A women's health assistant, in your language.

Women across East Africa deserve clear, judgment-free answers about their bodies. Ask Astura answers questions about periods, pregnancy, hygiene, and women's wellness in English, Amharic, and Somali.

Educational only. Always confidential. Always refers serious questions to a doctor, midwife, or pharmacist.

Ask Astura
Hi, I'm Astura. Ask me anything about periods, pregnancy, hygiene, or women's health. I answer in English, Amharic, or Somali. I'm here to help you understand your body, not to diagnose. For anything serious, please see a doctor or midwife.
